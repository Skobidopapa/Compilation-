Step By Step Process:

Gate/Entrance

Approach the designated gate or entrance for logging in to enter the campus.

STEP 1

Swipe your ID

Swipe your RFID ID card on the card reader at the entrance.

STEP 2

Check for Green Arrow

If a green arrow appears on the screen, it indicates successful login, and you can now enter the campus.

STEP 3

Log Out

Proceed to the designated gate or exit point for logging out when leaving the campus.

STEP 4

Swipe the ID Card Again

Swipe your RFID ID card on the reader to log out and confirm your exit from the school premises.

STEP 5

Check Log Info on TV Display

Your login and logout information will appear on a TV screen above, ensuring that the process was successful.
